Our Story

About AutomateMyPC.AI

AutomateMyPC.AI started with a simple frustration - spending too much time doing the same things on a computer that's supposedly more powerful than anything that existed a decade ago. If AI can write essays and generate images, why is organising a folder still a manual job? That question is what built this tool.

Our Mission

Mission

The mission behind AutomateMyPC.AI is simple: make PC automation accessible to every Windows user - regardless of their technical background or budget.

For years, tools like AutoHotkey, PowerShell, and Task Scheduler have given advanced users the ability to automate their computers. These are genuinely powerful tools - but they come with a serious barrier. You have to know how to code to use them properly. Most people don't. Most people shouldn't have to.

AutomateMyPC.AI removes that barrier entirely. If you can describe a task in plain English, your PC should be able to do it. That belief is the foundation of everything this tool is built on.

We built it for the student working on a deadline at midnight. For the office administrator managing hundreds of files every week. For anyone who has ever thought: "There has to be a better way to do this."

"If you can describe it, your PC can do it."

The core principle behind AutomateMyPC.AI - plain English is all you need.
